Corporate profile

  • The Government Integrated Telecommunications Network (GITN) is a network infrastructure to facilitate electronic flow of information for intra and inter-Government agencies, thereby enhancing the delivery of public services. In October 1995, the Cabinet approved the implementation of GITN to provide network and common services to the government agencies, on a privatisation basis.
  • GITN Sdn. Berhad (GSB) was incorporated in March 1996 to undertake the Government network implementation. At the same time the Government formed the GITN Steering Committee to set the direction and oversee its implementation. In May 1997, the Ministry of Energy, Water and Communications awarded the Value Added Network Operating License to GSB.
  • GSB has developed the nationwide network infrastructure and its services are currently being used by various Government agencies. We are proud of being part of the national aspiration to become a developed nation in the year 2020 and using GITN as a vehicle for change. The GITN enables electronic transfer and sharing of information across Government agencies, thereby enhancing the efficiency of service to the citizens at large. GITN is also a major component of the Government vision to realise the Electronic Government (e-Government) project under the Multimedia Super Corridor initiative.

Core Services

The network services provided by GSB utilise private Internet Protocol Virtual Private Network (IPVPN) technology developed on the latest network solutions standard for government agencies. It is designed as a private IPVPN network with huge backband bandwidth, i.e. 100 Gbps and nationwide access nodes based on Multi Protocol Label Switching (MPLS) technology.
